## 1. Executive Summary

**BUY | Fair Value: ~INR 6,200 | Current: INR 4,285 | Upside: 45%**

Tata Elxsi is India's most differentiated product engineering and design-led technology services company, serving as a critical infrastructure provider for the AI revolution, autonomous vehicles, and digital transformation. As a subsidiary of the Tata Group (44% holding), the company combines the trust of India's largest conglomerate with cutting-edge expertise in AI/ML, embedded systems, and design thinking. The stock has corrected **60% from its all-time high of INR 10,760**, driven by midcap IT sector rotation and AI disruption fears — creating an exceptional entry point. With 34% ROE, net-cash balance sheet, and a unique positioning at the intersection of AI, automotive, healthcare, and media technology, Tata Elxsi offers compelling long-term compounding potential.

---

## 2. Business Overview

Tata Elxsi operates two business segments:

**Software Development & Services (~85% of revenue):**
- **Automotive & Transportation** (~40%): AI platforms for autonomous driving, SDV middleware, EV systems, ADAS
- **Media & Communications** (~25%): OTT platforms, video analytics, 5G solutions
- **Healthcare & Life Sciences** (~20%): Digital health platforms, medical devices, regulatory compliance
- **Semiconductor & Others** (~15%): Chip design support, industrial IoT

**Systems Integration & Support (~15%):**
- Design-led product engineering services

**Flagship AI Platforms:**
- AIVA: AI-powered video analytics
- AutonomAI: Autonomous driving platform
- Digital Health Platform: Remote patient monitoring
- TETHER: Connected vehicle cloud platform
