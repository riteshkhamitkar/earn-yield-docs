## Rank #5 | Cloud Computing & IT Services | Score: 15.6/20

## 1. Executive Summary

**BUY | Fair Value: ~INR 1,850 | Current: INR 1,428 | Upside: 30%**

Coforge (formerly NIIT Technologies) is a mid-sized IT services company with a differentiated focus on cloud-native solutions, AI/ML services, and domain-specific expertise in travel, transportation, insurance, and banking. Following its acquisition by HCL founders and strategic investors, Coforge has undergone a transformation — rebranding, leadership refresh, and aggressive growth investments. The stock trades at a **28.8% discount to its all-time high** and at 32.6x trailing earnings, which is reasonable for a company delivering 18% revenue growth with improving margins. With nearly zero debt, strong cash generation, and a clear cloud-first strategy, Coforge offers attractive long-term compounding potential.

## 3. Industry Analysis

The Indian IT services industry (~$250B) continues to evolve:

- **Cloud migration**: $1T+ global enterprise workloads still on-premise
- **AI adoption**: Enterprise AI spend growing 30%+ annually
- **Digital transformation**: Post-COVID acceleration continues
- **Vendor consolidation**: Clients prefer fewer, deeper partnerships
- **India advantage**: Cost arbitrage + skill depth remains unmatched

Coforge's mid-market positioning allows it to offer **personalized service** that large IT giants cannot match, while being large enough to handle complex engagements.
