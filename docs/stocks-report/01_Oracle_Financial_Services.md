## 2. Business Overview

OFSS operates as a subsidiary of Oracle Global (Mauritius) Limited, providing mission-critical IT solutions to the financial services industry worldwide. The company operates through two segments:

- **Product Licenses & Related Activities (~65% of revenue)**: Core banking platform (Oracle FLEXCUBE), digital banking, lending & leasing, treasury management, payments, and risk management solutions
- **IT Solutions & Consulting Services (~35% of revenue)**: Implementation, customization, and ongoing support services

**Key Products:**
- Oracle FLEXCUBE Universal Banking
- Oracle Banking Digital Experience
- Oracle Financial Services Lending and Leasing
- Oracle Banking Payments
- Oracle Banking APIs
- Risk, finance, and regulatory compliance suite

**Geographic Revenue Split:** Americas (35%), Europe (25%), Middle East & Africa (20%), Asia Pacific (20%)

---

## 3. Industry Analysis

The global banking software market is valued at approximately $85 billion and growing at 8-10% CAGR. Key structural drivers include:

- **Digital transformation**: Banks moving from legacy mainframes to modern cloud-native platforms
- **Real-time payments**: FedNow, UPI, instant payment rails requiring infrastructure upgrades
- **Regulatory complexity**: Basel III/IV, IFRS 9, anti-money laundering (AML), DPDP Act compliance
- **Embedded finance**: Non-banks offering financial services through API-first platforms
- **AI integration**: Fraud detection, credit scoring, customer service automation

**OFSS sits at the center of all these trends.** Banks cannot operate without core banking software, and switching costs are astronomical — making this one of the most defensible software businesses globally.
