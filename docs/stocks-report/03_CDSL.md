## 2. Business Overview

CDSL provides depository services — the electronic storage and transfer of securities — operating under SEBI regulation as one of India's two licensed depositories.

**Revenue Streams:**
- **Transaction charges** (~40%): Fees on every buy/sell transaction
- **Annual issuer charges** (~20%): Fees from companies for maintaining shareholder records
- **Account maintenance** (~15%): Annual fees from demat account holders
- **IPO/corporate action services** (~15%): Processing IPO allotments, dividends, rights
- **KYC/eKYC services** (~10%): KYC registration and verification

**Key Metrics:**
- 15+ crore active demat accounts ( India's largest)
- 600+ depository participants (brokers, banks)
- 30,000+ companies with dematerialized securities

## 3. Industry Analysis

India's capital market infrastructure is experiencing unprecedented growth:

- **Demat account growth**: From 4 crore (2020) to 15+ crore (2025) — 30% CAGR
- **Financialization of savings**: Household equity allocation rising from 5% to 10%+
- ** SIP culture**: 5+ crore SIP accounts; monthly inflows of INR 24,000+ crore
- **New asset classes**: Corporate bonds, municipal bonds, REITs, InvITs, gold bonds
- **Regulatory push**: Mandatory demat for more securities; T+1 settlement
- **Internationalization**: GIFT City, FPI registrations, India bond index inclusion

**CDSL is the toll booth on India's financial highway.** Every transaction, every new account, every IPO flows through the depository system.

## 4. Competitive Landscape

| Player | Demat Accounts | Market Share | Notes |
|--------|---------------|--------------|-------|
| **CDSL** | 15+ crore | ~65% | Cost leader, retail-focused |
| NSDL | 8+ crore | ~35% | Institution-focused, older |

**CDSL has won the retail market** through lower costs and better broker relationships. The duopoly structure ensures rational pricing and stable margins.

**Barriers to Entry:**
- SEBI license required (no new licenses issued since 1996)
- INR 500+ crore minimum net worth requirement
- Technology infrastructure serving 15 crore accounts
- Regulatory compliance and audit requirements
- Switching costs for 600+ depository participants
