## Rank #10 | Software Product Engineering | Score: 15.0/20

## 1. Executive Summary

**BUY | Fair Value: ~INR 7,200 | Current: INR 5,826 | Upside: 24%**

Persistent Systems is India's leading software product engineering company, partnering with global enterprises and software product companies to design, develop, and modernize their technology products. With a unique focus on **product engineering** (vs. generic IT services), Persistent has built deep expertise in cloud-native development, data engineering, AI/ML integration, and customer experience transformation. The company serves 75%+ of the Forbes 100 software companies and has emerged as a preferred partner for digital transformation initiatives. After a **27% correction from recent highs**, the stock trades at 43x trailing earnings — a premium justified by superior growth (21% revenue CAGR), exceptional ROE (22%), and a net-cash balance sheet. Persistent is a high-quality compounder in the digital transformation megatrend.

## 2. Business Overview

Persistent operates as a product engineering-focused IT services company:

**Service Lines:**
- **Software Product Engineering** (~45%): Full-lifecycle product development for software companies
- **Cloud & Infrastructure** (~25%): Cloud migration, DevOps, platform engineering
- **Data & Analytics** (~15%): Data engineering, AI/ML, business intelligence
- **Customer Experience** (~10%): UX/UI design, digital experience platforms
- **Intelligent Automation** (~5%): RPA, process automation

**Industry Verticals:**
- Software & Hi-Tech (~40%)
- Banking & Financial Services (~25%)
- Healthcare & Life Sciences (~20%)
- Industrials (~15%)

**Key Differentiator**: Unlike traditional IT services, Persistent focuses on **product engineering** — helping companies build software products rather than just maintaining enterprise systems.
