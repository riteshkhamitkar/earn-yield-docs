## 1. Executive Summary

**BUY | Fair Value: ~INR 1,030 | Current: INR 762 | Upside: 35%**

KPIT Technologies is India's most focused and differentiated automotive software company, positioned at the epicenter of the three most transformative trends in the auto industry: electric vehicles (EVs), autonomous driving, and software-defined vehicles (SDVs). The company provides mission-critical embedded software, AI/ML solutions, and digital engineering services to 8 of the top 10 global OEMs. After a **60.5% drawdown from its all-time high of INR 1,929**, KPIT trades at 32.7x trailing earnings — a reasonable valuation for a company growing earnings at 35%+ CAGR. With a strong moat built on domain-specific IP, deep OEM relationships, and the acquisition of Technica Engineering (Germany), KPIT offers exceptional long-term compounding potential. The company is net-debt-free with strong cash generation.

---

## 2. Business Overview

KPIT Technologies provides embedded software, AI, and digital solutions exclusively for the automobile and mobility sector. The company serves:

**Core Service Lines:**
- **Autonomous Driving & ADAS** (~25%): System engineering, functional safety, AI-based perception
- **Electric Vehicle Solutions** (~30%): BMS platform, charging platform, VCU, FCEV solutions
- **Vehicle OS & Architecture** (~25%): E/E architecture, middleware, AUTOSAR integration
- **Connected Solutions** (~15%): Cloud, edge analytics, OTA updates, cybersecurity
- **Diagnostics & Aftersales** (~5%): Virtual engineering, integrated diagnostics

**Client Base:** 8 of top 10 global OEMs including Volkswagen Group, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, Ford, and Stellantis

**Geographic Mix:** Europe (45%), Americas (35%), Asia (20%)

---

## 3. Industry Analysis

The automotive software market is projected to grow from $35 billion (2024) to $85 billion by 2030 (16% CAGR), driven by:

- **Software-Defined Vehicles**: Modern cars contain 100+ ECUs running 100 million lines of code
- **EV transition**: Every EV requires complex battery management, thermal management, and charging software
- **ADAS/Autonomous driving**: Level 2+ autonomy requires sensor fusion, AI processing, and functional safety
- **Connected cars**: V2X communication, OTA updates, digital services monetization
- **Regulatory mandates**: Euro 7 emissions, pedestrian safety, cybersecurity regulations

**KPIT is embedded in all these layers.** As cars become "computers on wheels," the software content per vehicle is expected to grow from $500 to $3,000+ by 2030.
