## MONITORING FRAMEWORK

We recommend reviewing positions quarterly but **trading only on fundamental deterioration**, not price movement:

### Hold Triggers (Do NOT sell unless):
- Moat erosion (competitive disruption)
- Governance red flags (fraud, promoter issues)
- Balance sheet deterioration (debt spike, cash burn)
- Structural industry decline
- Valuation exceeding 50x forward earnings for 2+ consecutive years

### Add Triggers (Consider increasing position):
- Drawdown increases to >50% with intact fundamentals
- Dividend yield >8% (for dividend stocks)
- New product/platform launches
- Accretive acquisitions
- Market share gains
